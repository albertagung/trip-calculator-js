Simple Trip Calculator

Ever want to have a good trip with your family or friends?

This trip calculator is designed to help you plan your trip to anywhere.

Now you don't have to worry thinking about:

  • How much you will spend on gasoline price
  • How much you will spend on car maintenance
  • How much fuel you need to arrive at the destination
  • The right time to refuel your car along the trip
  • Even count your odometer after the trip!

What will this trip calculator do?

  • Calculate total distance traveled
  • Calculate fuel needed each trip
  • Calculate how many refuel needed for each trip
  • Calculate how much you spent for maintenance and fuel for each trip
  • Calculate when you should do refuel
  • Calculate your odometer after each trip
